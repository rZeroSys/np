================================================================================
OVERVIEW
================================================================================
The Kastle Back to Work Barometer tracks office building access data from 2,600
buildings and 41,000 businesses across 47 states. Data reflects unique authorized
user entries in each market relative to a pre-COVID baseline, averaged weekly.
