UtilityiWe convert consumption to annual costs using ZIP-specific rates from NREL's Utility Rate Database.

For electricity, we multiply kWh by the rate, then add about 10% for distribution, taxes, and surcharges. Demand charges are based on peak kW times 12 months, with a 26% markup for ratchet clauses and seasonal variations. Gas and fuel oil get a 10% markup. Steam rates are all-inclusive.
